About

Hi! I am Judith.

I am interested in finding new ways to approach problems and in using them for solutions. Accurate research and comunication with professionals and non-professionals play an important role in my design development. I am a quick learner. When it comes to new programs, I know how and where to find solutions when I have doubts. I have a keen interest in technologies such as VR, AR, AI as well as programmes such as Unreal Engine and Unity, which I would like to build on.
