Natoure

“NATOURE” is a modular infrastructure designed to invite tourists and locals to spend more time in the South Tyrolean landscape. The individual parts consist of a basic structure that can be adapted to the surrounding environment with additional elements. The basic structure would be suitable for a panoramic viewpoint in combination with a balcony, and with a dock it could stand by a lake.
